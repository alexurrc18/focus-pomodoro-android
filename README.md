## Features
* **Custom Pomodoro Timer:** Set your own work and break intervals. Everyone works differently, so make it fit your rhythm.
* **Social Media Blocker:** The core of the app. It uses Android's `AccessibilityService` to catch you when you try to open Instagram, TikTok, or other apps during a focus session, sending you back to the home screen.
* **Task List:** Keep your goals organized with a local To-Do list, powered by Room Database.
* **Look & Feel:** Custom notifications, sound effects, and vibrations ensure you never miss a transition between work and break times. For every focus session completed, your streak increases and you are rewarded with confetti.
